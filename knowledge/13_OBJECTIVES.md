# Scope

Every entry below is extracted verbatim from a frozen CRT. Nothing
here is new reasoning. Each entry states its **Level** (Stakeholder or
Ecosystem) explicitly, per the standing documentation rule.

Voice note, preserved as-is, not smoothed over: stakeholder-level
Objectives are written in first person, matching the Wall's own voice.
The Ecosystem-level Objective is written in third-person analyst
language. This distinction was deliberate throughout the investigation
and is preserved here rather than normalized to one style.

---

## Ecosystem Objective

**Level:** Ecosystem

**Statement:** Reliably convert existing qualified supply and demand
into qualified opportunities.

**Note (preserved from source):** this is the Ecosystem Constraint,
restated positively — not an independently invented aspiration. It
states what would have to be true for the Constraint to no longer hold.

## Seller Objective

**Level:** Stakeholder — Seller (Property Owner)

**Statement:** "Sell my property for a good outcome, with confidence
in the process."

**Note (preserved from source):** Seller's Objective is bounded to a
single transaction, not a perpetual state — a structural difference
from Broker's ongoing-practice framing, not an inconsistency.

## Buyer Objective

**Level:** Stakeholder — Buyer

confidence in the price and the process."
