# Scope

here is new reasoning. Each entry states its **Level** (Stakeholder or
Ecosystem) explicitly. Same voice note as 13_OBJECTIVES.md:
stakeholder-level Needs are first person; Ecosystem-level Needs are

## Ecosystem Needs

**Level:** Ecosystem

**Need B:** Qualified reach — the right supply must reach the right
demand, not necessarily the widest possible audience.

**Need C:** Trusted representation — effort and claims must be
attributable to a specific, verifiable, accountable party.

**Note (preserved from source):** Needs B and C are not new
inventions — they are the same pattern already present, independently,
in all three stakeholder Clouds below, recognized once at the level it
actually belongs. **Needs B and C are not in conflict with each
other** — this was an explicit, corrected finding after an earlier
draft stated the conflict at this level. The conflict lives entirely
at the Prerequisite (Want) level; see 15_PREREQUISITES.md.
